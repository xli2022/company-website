How to delete your Draft Wars account (Android)

The following steps are applicable to Draft Wars: PvP Tower Defense downloaded from Google Play.

Step 1: Open the settings menu and press the Delete Account button

Step 2: Read the warnings and press Delete

All of your player data will be deleted from our server instantly and permanently. We may still retain the receipt numbers of your In-App Purchases made via Google Play.
